High Quality FIBC Bags

Pelax Industries is a premier manufacturer and exporter of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC), widely known as Bulk Bags or Jumbo Bags. Our packaging solutions are designed to meet the rigorous demands of various industries, including agriculture, chemicals, construction, and mining.

We offer a wide range of FIBC bags, including standard 4-loop bags, cross-corner loop bags, and specialized bags for hazardous materials. utilizing state-of-the-art manufacturing processes to ensure strength, durability, and compliance with international safety standards.
